About Me

I’ve always loved writing and much like my book’s protagonist, Jonny, I’ve been fascinated by the mysterious world of dreams, especially lucid dreams. Since being a little girl, I’ve had vivid dreams that I jotted down in my dream journal kept under the bed. Some of these entries have inspired the fantasy world Jonny travels to. They were like little puzzle pieces that I assembled to create a full picture.
However, my inspiration came also from other sources. Growing up, I was immersed in Greek myths, fantasy books and Disney films.
The idea of the Sun Disc originates from an old Inca Legend I heard about during my 2015 trip to Peru.
Originally from Italy, I now call London my home. I came to London with the sole intention of earning my master's degree, but 16 years have passed since then. When I'm not writing, you can find me lecturing on fashion. Although my job takes up most of my time, I also enjoy being in nature, exploring the city, travelling or getting lost in a good book at night. "Jonny & the Mystery of the Sun Disc" is my debut novel, and I hope you enjoy reading it as much as I enjoyed writing it.
